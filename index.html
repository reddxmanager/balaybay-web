Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 27, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the mobile applications "Gubbins Gacha", "Hatchbytes", "Spin It", "One Thing", "Whose Turn?" and "Did I?" developed by us.

Data Collection: Most of our applications do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). No account registration is required.

Did I? - Additional Data

The "Did I?" app uses Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver reminder notifications. This requires:

  • A randomly generated device identifier (not linked to your identity)
  • A push notification token provided by Google
  • Your device's timezone offset
  • Reminder times and labels you configure

This data is stored securely on Firebase servers and is used solely to deliver your scheduled reminders. We do not share this data with third parties. You can delete your data by uninstalling the app.
